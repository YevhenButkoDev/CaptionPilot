Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last updated: September 2025

Introduction

CaptionPilot ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered social media automation application.

Quick Summary

We only collect the data necessary to provide our services. We don't sell your data, and we use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. You have full control over your data and can request deletion at any time.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Instagram and Pinterest account metadata (User IDs, connected Page IDs)
  • Account access tokens (stored securely and rotated according to platform policies)
  • Profile information necessary for content publishing

2. Content Data

  • Images and media files you upload for processing and publishing
  • Captions, descriptions, and text content you create or generate
  • Project organization data and content categorization
  • Post scheduling preferences and timing data

3. Usage Data

  • Application usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Performance metrics and analytics data
  • Error logs and technical diagnostics
  • Device information and application version data

4. AI Processing Data

  • Content analysis data for AI caption generation
  • Image processing parameters and optimization settings
  • Brand voice and tone preferences for content generation

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Content Publishing: Create and publish posts/reels on your behalf via Instagram and Pinterest APIs
  • AI Services: Generate captions, optimize content, and provide intelligent automation features
  • Content Management: Organize, store, and manage your media assets and projects
  • Scheduling: Automate post scheduling and timing optimization

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI algorithms and features
  • Optimize application performance and user experience
  • Develop new features and capabilities
  • Provide technical support and troubleshooting

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data remains private and is only used to provide our services.

Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Platform APIs: With Instagram and Pinterest APIs as necessary for content publishing
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services for cloud hosting, analytics, and technical infrastructure
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice)

Data Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication for all data access
  • Token Management: Access tokens are securely stored and automatically rotated per platform policies
  • Regular Audits: Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Infrastructure Security: Secure cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade protection

Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours and take immediate action to secure the system and prevent further unauthorized access.

Data Retention

Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after deletion
  • Content Data: Retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for service improvement
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time. We will permanently delete your personal information within 30 days of your request, except where retention is required by law.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your personal data

Account Controls

  • Disconnect social media accounts at any time
  • Modify or delete scheduled posts
  • Control AI processing preferences
  • Manage notification settings

Third-Party Services

Platform Integrations

Our application integrates with Instagram and Pinterest APIs. These platforms have their own privacy policies and data practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • Instagram Privacy Policy
  • Pinterest Privacy Policy

Third-Party Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services to understand how our application is used. These services collect anonymized usage data and are bound by their own privacy policies.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure that any international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and use appropriate safeguards to protect your information.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying a notice in the application

Your continued use of our service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
